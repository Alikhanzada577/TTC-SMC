Energy

With over 20 years of combined experience in the building services industry, TTC is well-equipped to serve a wide range of clients in the solar energy sector. We support projects at all stages—from initial conception and design development to construction, maintenance, and optimization of existing solar energy systems. Our services include technical advisory, mechanical completion, and commissioning. TTC has successfully managed and protected solar energy projects by developing industry-specific methods and tools to monitor, control, and safeguard our clients’ solar installations and infrastructure.

Solar Power System Installation & Commissioning
