Energy

Since 2017, TTC has been well-equipped to serve a wide range of clients in the solar energy sector. We support projects at all stages—from initial conception and design development to construction, maintenance, and optimization of existing solar energy systems. Our services include technical advisory, mechanical completion, and commissioning. TTC has successfully managed and protected solar energy projects by developing industry-specific methods and tools to monitor, control, and safeguard our clients’ solar installations and infrastructure.

Solar Power System Installation & Commissioning

At The Throne Chasers SMC-Private Limited, we are dedicated to driving sustainable energy solutions and empowering communities through our comprehensive solar system installation services. With a commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we provide tailored solar energy solutions that meet the unique needs of residential, commercial, and industrial clients.
