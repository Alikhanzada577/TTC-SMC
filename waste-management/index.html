Waste Recyle

With over 20 years of combined experience in the waste management industry, TTC is well-equipped to serve a wide range of clients in the recycling sector. Our services include technical advisory, process completion, and commissioning. TTC has successfully managed and protected recycling projects by developing industry-specific methods and tools to monitor, control, and safeguard our clients’ waste recycling processes and infrastructure.
